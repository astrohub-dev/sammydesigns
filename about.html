Welcome! We are delighted you found our brand!

SAMMYLEE DESIGNER is a top fashion brand in Nigeria located at Banex plaza Plot 741 Aminu Kano Cres, Wuse 2, Abuja, Federal Capital Territory.

Through fashion, our goal is to make you feel beautiful, unique, and boost your confidence while embracing and appreciating the rich African culture in any environment you may find yourself.
All clothes and accessories are made with various type of premium African print fabrics, from African Wax also known as Ankara, to Bogolan also known as Mud Cloth.
